That is every human being on these planet Eagerly want. But what about bad days in life? Where we very miserable, bankrupt, having very bad health, some get cheated badly or With full of problems that anybody doesn't want. And second are who get depressed, pack of their mind with full of negativity and at the last, end themself. At the end, perspective of person towards his/her life decides his destiny.
